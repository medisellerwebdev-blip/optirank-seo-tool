# 🚀 OptiRank AI - Comprehensive Feature Guide

OptiRank AI is an all-in-one, AI-powered SEO optimization platform designed to streamline your search engine ranking workflow. Below is a detailed breakdown of all available features.

---

## 📊 1. AI Dashboard
**Purpose**: Your central command center for project oversight and SEO performance tracking.
- **Health Monitoring**: View a high-level SEO health score for your connected projects.
- **Activity Feed**: Track recent content generations, keyword searches, and technical audits.
- **Insights**: AI-driven summaries of what needs immediate attention on your website.

## ✍️ 2. Multimodal Content Generator
**Purpose**: Generate high-conversion, SEO-optimized articles and landing page copy using AI.
- **Smart Context**: Generates content based on keywords, reference URLs, text notes, or even images.
- **Full SEO Stack**: Every article comes with:
    - Optimized Headings (H1-H6).
    - Meta Titles & Meta Descriptions.
    - Automated **FAQ Schema (JSON-LD)**.
    - Internal Link Suggestions.
- **Export Options**: Copy raw HTML for CMS (WordPress/Shopify) or export as a Markdown (`.md`) file.

## 🔍 3. Deep Keyword Research
**Purpose**: Identify the most profitable and low-competition search terms for your niche.
- **Key Metrics**:
    - **Search Volume**: How many people are searching for the term.
    - **Keyword Difficulty (KD)**: How hard it is to rank in the top 10.
    - **CPC & Intent**: Understand the commercial value and user intent (Informational, Transactional).
- **Clusters & Variations**: Discover related long-tail keywords and topic clusters to build topical authority.

## 🛠️ 4. Technical SEO Auditor
**Purpose**: Audit any live URL to find and fix technical issues that prevent ranking.
- **On-Page Audit**: Analyzes meta tags, heading structures, favicons, and site performance.
- **Health Score**: A real-time 0-100 score based on critical errors and warnings.
- **Robots.txt Generator**: Instantly generate or optimize your site's robots.txt file based on specific SEO needs.

## 🖼️ 5. Media SEO (Vision AI)
**Purpose**: Optimize images to improve accessibility and rank in Google Image Search.
- **Vision Recognition**: Upload an image, and AI "sees" it to generate:
    - Optimized Filenames (SEO-friendly slugs).
    - Accessibility-driven **Alt Text**.
    - Visible Captions.
- **Rich Snippets**: Generates **ImageObject Schema** to help Google index your media effectively.

## 🔗 6. Backlink Opportunity Finder
**Purpose**: Build site authority by finding high-quality link-building prospects.
- **Domain Prospecting**: Identifies websites in your niche with high Domain Rating (DR) and traffic.
- **Guest Post Leads**: Finds sites that explicitly accept guest contributions.
- **Outreach Templates**: Professionally written email templates for Guest Posting and Broken Link Building.

---

### **How to get started?**
1.  **Identify** your target niche in **Keyword Research**.
2.  **Generate** premium content in the **Content Generator**.
3.  **Optimize** your site's technical foundation with the **Technical SEO** auditor.
4.  **Enhance** your site's authority using the **Backlink Finder**.
